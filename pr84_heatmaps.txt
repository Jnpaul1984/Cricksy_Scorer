### Week 5 Feature: AI Heatmap Overlays

**Status:** Previously implemented and verified

#### Implementation Details

**Backend Service:** `pitch_heatmap_generator.py`
- **PitchHeatmapGenerator** class with static methods
- **Zone System:** 11 PitchZone enums with ZONE_BOUNDARIES mapping
  - off_side_short, off_side_full, off_side_yorker
  - middle_short, middle_full, middle_yorker
  - leg_side_short, leg_side_full, leg_side_yorker
  - wide_off, wide_leg
- **Coordinate System:** 0-100 scale (X: leg→middle→off, Y: bowler→stumps)
- **Heatmap Types:** HeatmapType.RUNS, DISMISSALS, BOWLER_RELEASE, BATTING_CONTACT

**Core Methods:**
- `generate_batter_scoring_heatmap()` - Where player scores runs
- `generate_dismissal_heatmap()` - Where player gets dismissed
- `generate_bowler_release_heatmap()` - Bowler release point consistency
- `analyze_batter_profile()` - Comprehensive spatial scoring analysis
- `analyze_bowler_profile()` - Delivery zone effectiveness
- `analyze_matchup()` - Batter vs bowler spatial analysis

**API Endpoints:** `pitch_heatmaps.py`
- GET `/heatmaps/players/{id}/batting-heatmap`
- GET `/heatmaps/players/{id}/dismissal-heatmap`
- GET `/heatmaps/bowlers/{id}/release-zones`
- GET `/heatmaps/matchups/{batter_id}/vs/{bowler_id}`
- GET `/heatmaps/games/{id}/team-{side}/heatmap-summary`

**Frontend Component:**
- `HeatmapOverlayPanel.vue` - SVG-based pitch visualization with color-coded zones
- Interactive hover for zone details
- Field position selection

**Data Models:**
- PitchHeatmap
- HeatmapDataPoint
- BatterProfile
- BowlerProfile
- MatchupAnalysis
