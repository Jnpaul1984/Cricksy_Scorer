## 1. Welcome

**What is Cricksy?**

Cricksy is a modern cricket scoring and analytics platform designed for schools, clubs, and academies. It lets you score matches ball-by-ball, track player stats over time, and unlock AI-powered insights to help coaches and analysts improve performance.

**Why you were invited**

You're here because we trust your cricket knowledge and want your honest feedback. Whether you're a coach, analyst, or org admin, your perspective is invaluable in shaping what Cricksy becomes.

> **Please be brutally honest.** Tell us what's confusing, what's slow, what's broken, and what you wish existed. No feedback is too small or too harsh. We'd rather hear it now than after launch.
